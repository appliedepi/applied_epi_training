About

Applied Epi is a nonprofit organization that supports frontline public health practitioners through accessible tools, training, and support.

We are a global team of 170 applied epidemiologists and public health practitioners with extensive ground-level experience.

Our free Epidemiologist R Handbook is a vital resource for over 450,000 people worldwide.

We offer a comprehensive ecosystem of training, tools, and support:

Training:
Live courses
Tutorials
Case studies

Tools:
Epi R Handbook

Support:
